Allow me to introduce myself...

I am currently an undergraduate for a Bachelor of Education (Secondary) and Bachelor of Arts (Technology) at the Australian Cathplic University (ACU) on the Strathfield campus. As a student in highschool I found my passion for practical subjects such as woodwork and textiles. Working on projects allowed me take my mind off of the rest of the world and focus only on my own skills and goals in order to make a quality product. I felt the pull towards teaching from a young age, so finding great value and reward in TAS secured this choice for my career pathway. This portfolio contains more information about me, my goals, and what I believe is required of teachers in my field in order to convey quality learning to my future students. I will analyse my current abilities and outline ambition checkpoints that I will take efforts to meet. I will also look at the NESA Technology Mandatory Syllabus to assess my capability in meeting the standards for my intended line of work. Where gaps are found I will make SMART goals that have realistic and orderly time frames for achievement.
